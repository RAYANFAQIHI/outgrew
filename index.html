About Us

The best way for you to start a clean, waste-free world. The OutGrow application helps you, using modern technologies, to speed up and facilitate the process of waste disposal. With just your phone, you can manage all your trash.

Maps showing the locations of waste collection points or local recycling centres
Recycling hazardous waste Do not worry about dealing with hazardous waste. Recycle it in safe ways
Smart Delivery: Artificial intelligence can be applied to optimize delivery timings and choose the best routes based on estimated time and actual conditions.
General Recycling: An easier way to collect and recycle general waste
Career opportunities available for volunteers and the unemployed
